Our Products

Crop Fertilizer

Optimize fertilizer application by analyzing soil nutrient levels, crop nutrient requirements, and environmental factors, minimizing wastage and environmental impact.

Crop Price Forecasting:

Forecast market prices based on historical trends, demand-supply dynamics, and external factors like weather events and policy changes, assisting farmers in marketing and pricing strategies.

Crop Water Management:

Predict water requirements based on crop type, growth stage, soil moisture levels, and weather forecasts, ensuring efficient irrigation practices and water conservation.

Weather

A weather application integrated with the system provides real-time weather updates and forecasts, enabling farmers to adjust their farming practices promptly in response to changing weather conditions.

Crop Yield Prediction:

Accurately forecast crop yields based on historical data and current environmental conditions, enabling farmers to plan planting schedules and optimize harvest expectations.

Help AI/chatbot

A chatbot for agriculture development assists farmers by providing real-time information on crop management, pest control, weather forecasts, soil health, and market prices. It enhances agricultural practices through accessible, instant support, improving productivity and decision-making.

Crop Recommendation:

Recommend suitable crop varieties based on soil quality, climate suitability, and market demand, aiding farmers in making informed planting decisions.
